About Me

My name is Samuel Adeniyi

Samuel OgooluwaAdeniyi was born on the 11th October 2009 in Lagos-Nigeria in the Year of “Glory to Glory in the Redeemed Christian Church of God”. Samuel who hails from Ogun state is the eldest in the family of three children.

At seven years, he recited the first scriptures, Psalm 103:1-22 to thank God for life, because his early years were challenging, falling in and out of sickness. This outstanding performance elicited some financial rewards from parishioners out of which he sowed with the advice of His parents. Eventually thegift of God became multiplied upon young Samuel who had since then recited couples of scriptures.

In December 2017 Holy Congress, having observed the wisdom of God upon Samuel, His father prayed for more grace upon him to recite the longest chapter in the bible Psalm 119:1-end. Amazingly, in answer to Samuel’s dad prayers he dazzled the entire congregation as he recited like a colossus, the longest chapter in the bible Psalm 119:1-end (176 Verses) during the Children’s Holy Ghost Service in Service in Simara Camp. This extra ordinary performance of Samuel eight years old had not only endeared him in Christian community, it has also been the talk. Read More
